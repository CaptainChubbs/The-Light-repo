    • Health Risk Assessments

      A health risk assessment (HRA), sometimes known as a health risk appraisal or health assessment, is a questionnaire that evaluates lifestyle factors and health risks of an individual. In both use cases, a typical HRA includes:

      • Blood Glucose Checks
      • Blood Pressure Checks
      • Cholesterol Level Checks
      • Questionnaire
      • Risk Score

    Health Promotion Talks

    We offer health education or health promotion. This is a combination of learning experiences designed to facilitate voluntary actions conductive to health improvement. Health education strategies are tailored to the target population. We peresent information to target populations on particular health topics, including the health/threats they face, and provide tools to build capacity and support behavioural change in an appropriate setting.

    Health Assessment

    Health assessments can include condition-specific questionnaires, symptom checkers, and eligibility checkers that help prospective patients self-identify their need for care. These assessments are shorter than a comprehensive HRA used in population health and wellness programs, and typically focus on one condition or health concern.
